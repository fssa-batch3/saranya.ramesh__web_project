Guindy National Park

Description

This Marine Kingdom is the fourth in the many kingdoms unveiled by VGP. This is India’s first and largest walk-through aquarium housing everything from small to big aquatic animals. The animals here are housed in life-size tanks and over-head aquariums to give you a larger-than-life experience. So welcome to VGP Marine Kingdom, and while you are here, don’t forget to meet the sharks.
