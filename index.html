Mystery Room

About:

Mystery Rooms is a challenging race against the clock where the sole motive is to get yourself out of a themed space before time kicks you! Imagine being locked in a thrill space with your team of 2-8 people and you have exact 60 minutes to use logic, find clues, unravel a mystery and prove the power of team work to conclude a mission.

Suggested duration:One Hour

Timing : 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Review :

Right place to spend your precious time with your loved ones, friends or family. The rooms are very well done. And price is very reasonable. Must do if you loves to solve the puzzles and escape.
